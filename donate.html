Our Impact Timeline

2021 - Foundation

Established NutriBites with the mission to provide nutritious, shelf-stable meals to communities in need. Started our first food drive in Yangon.

2022 - Recipe Development

Launched our first collection of 50 non-perishable recipes. Distributed over 10,000 meal kits to families affected by natural disasters.

2023 - Regional Expansion

Expanded operations to three new regions. Reached 50,000+ people with our programs.

2024 - Recognition

Received the Green Charity Award for our sustainable approach to fighting hunger. Launched our digital recipe platform serving 100,000+ monthly users.
