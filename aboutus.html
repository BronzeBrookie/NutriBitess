About NutriBites

The journey of NutriBites began not with a grand plan, but with a simple, yet powerful idea to provide nutritious food solutions in times of need.

Our Story:

The Birth of NutriBites

"At NutriBites, our journey began with a simple yet powerful observation: communities affected by natural disasters often struggle to access consistent and nutritious food. Witnessing these challenges firsthand deeply moved us. We saw families and individuals facing not only the immediate aftermath of disasters but also the ongoing struggle to maintain their health and well-being through proper nutrition."

"Driven by a shared desire to make a meaningful difference, our team set out to find a practical and sustainable solution. One day, during a brainstorming session, inspiration struck in the most unexpected way. As we gathered around the table, one of our members took a bite of a homemade cookie. With a smile, they remarked on how surprisingly nutritious and satisfying it was—a true 'biteful' of goodness. In that moment, a bright idea pierced through the conversation and sparked our imaginations."

Who We Are

NutriBites was founded in 2022 with a simple mission: to publish nutritious & non-perishable food recipes.

We are a small student community established by four year-8 students from Myanmar, efforting out the best we can to develop recipes that are not only nutritious but also shelf-stable and easy to prepare in any situation for disaster-affected people by creating this website.

We believe that access to proper nutrition is a basic human right, and we're committed to take this task.

Our Mission

Our Vision

We envision a world where the immediate aftermath of a disaster doesn't compound the suffering with widespread hunger.

Our goal is to empower communities with the knowledge and resources to access nourishing meals when they need them most. We see a future where no family has to choose between feeding their children and other essential needs during crises.

Through innovation and collaboration, we aim to create sustainable food solutions that can be adapted to any environment or situation.
